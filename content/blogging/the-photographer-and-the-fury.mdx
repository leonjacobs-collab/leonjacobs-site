This is [a lovely Peta-Pixel write-up](https://petapixel.com/2026/04/03/how-a-photographer-captured-the-artemis-ii-launch-with-14-cameras/) of how photographer Steven Madow set up a camera chain to capture the most insane pictures of the Artemis launch on April 1st. Including this rectangle of insanity, that perfectly captures the power of those RS-25D rocket motors, all pointed at the floor, with the rocket system pointed at the sky.

![Steven Madow's image of Artemis rockets firing during launch.](https://petapixel.com/assets/uploads/2026/04/4633972.jpg)

I love how much preparation and thought went into this shoot. And how creatively he thought about how to trigger the sequence.
